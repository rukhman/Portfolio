more about me

I was born in 1993 in the small town of Russia - Dzerzhinsk. Even at school, it turned out that I prefer school-subjects where a technical mindset is needing. This played an important role in the choice of the university. All my relatives on my father's side were railway workers. I decided to continue the dynasty. In 2010, I entered the St. Petersburg State University of Railways named after Emperor Alexander I, the Faculty of "Automation and Intelligent Technologies". In 2015, i defended my diploma with honors. After studying, I started my career in Nizhny Novgorod. I got my first position as an electrical engineer in August 2015. My responsibilities included: maintenance, repairs, installation of new electrical appliances, and maintenance of documentation. A year later, I received a new position as a technologist of the 2nd category and was transferred to the main building of the railway managerment. My duties included: diagnostics and control of electrical appliances, as well as remote assistance by troubleshooting. In 2018, I finished my career at Russian Railways, where my total length of service was 3 years. At the end of the same year, I realized my long-held dream, namely got a national visa to Germany to improve my language skills. In 2020, i returned to Russia width "TestDaF" certificate, with which I could enter any university in German-speaking countries. After this long trip to Germany, i realized my another dream, namely retrained as a "Frontend Developer" at the online university "SkillBox". Thanks to a well-chosen training structure, I managed to master a huge part of this specialty in less than a year. Since 2021, I have been working as a developer in a large Russian company that is engaged in large-scale projects in various fields of programming. I really like to write clean code in JavaScript, investigate and understand new aspects of programming. I also have hobbies, among which I spent most of my time learning German and English and sometimes go out for a walk to contemplate nature. Of course, this is not all that I could tell about myself. I am always ready to answer your clarifying questions.
